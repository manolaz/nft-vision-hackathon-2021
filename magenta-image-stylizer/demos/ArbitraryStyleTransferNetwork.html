mi.ArbitraryStyleTransferNetwork

Style transfer wraps around Reiichiro Nakano's TensorFlow.js port of Ghiasi et al.'s arbitrary stylization model. The demo below performs stylization on a content/style image pair by

  • calling the predictStyleParameters method with the style image to obtain a style representation, and
  • calling the stylize method with the content image and the style representation to perform stylization.
